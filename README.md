# cloud-itonami-iso3166-mlt

Open ISO 3166 Blueprint for **MLT**: Republic of Malta.

Independent public-sector market-entry & procurement-compliance service
for an already-incorporated operator entering public contracts in Malta.

## Official surface

- Procurement: ePPS (electronic Public Procurement System) of the Department of Contracts
- Business/tax: MFSA/Registry of Companies + VAT
