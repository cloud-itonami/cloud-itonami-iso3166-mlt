# Operator Guide — MLT

Portal: ePPS (Electronic Public Procurement System), governed by the
Department of Contracts (Dipartiment tal-Kuntratti) within the
Ministry for Finance's portfolio under the Public Procurement
Regulations (S.L. 601.03). Entity: Malta Business Registry (MBR)
incorporation (Companies Act, Cap. 386) via the BAROS portal +
Commissioner for Revenue tax record (Income Tax Act, Cap. 123).
Engagements bidding into an urgency-invoked call also clear
Regulation 116(3) / Regulation 116(5) proviso's own 15-day minimum
tender-notice-period floor before a filing can proceed. Human-gated
filing only.

1. Confirm MBR incorporation and Commissioner for Revenue registration.
2. Intake against ePPS (Electronic Public Procurement System), the
   Department of Contracts' e-tendering portal.
3. If the engagement declares `:urgency-invoked? true`, confirm the
   declared notice period meets the Regulation 116 fifteen-day floor.
4. Human-gated filing only -- `:filing/draft`/`:filing/submit` always
   require approval, at every rollout phase.
