# Business Model — Republic of Malta

## Offer
- Department of Contracts (Dipartiment tal-Kuntratti), within the
  portfolio of the Ministry for Finance (MFIN) -- Competent Authority
  for Public Procurement, Public Procurement Regulations (S.L. 601.03,
  originally Legal Notice 352 of 2016); ePPS (Electronic Public
  Procurement System) e-tendering portal (see `src/marketentry/facts.cljc`)
- Malta Business Registry (MBR, established under S.L. 595.27, led by
  the Registrar of Companies) incorporation record, Companies Act
  (Cap. 386), via the BAROS (Business Automation Registry Online
  System) portal
- Commissioner for Revenue tax registration, Income Tax Act (Cap. 123)
- Public Procurement Regulations, Regulation 116(3) / Regulation
  116(5) proviso minimum-tender-notice-period gate (as substituted by
  Legal Notice 195 of 2019) -- bars a filing/submit from proceeding
  when the engagement declares it is bidding into an urgency-invoked
  call whose own notice period falls short of the regulatory 15-day
  floor
- EU member — no national-content quota; EU single-market access

## Trust Controls
- `:filing/submit` never automated
- fabricated claims are HARD holds
